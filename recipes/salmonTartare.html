Salmon Tartare Recipe

Description

This delicious salmon tatare is perfect for an appetizer or a summer snack! This light meal takes a total of 35 minutes to make and serves 4. With varying ingredients used for taste, this recipe won't leave you disappointed!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 Skinless salmon
  • 4 teaspoons Finely chopped shallot
  • 2 tablespoons White wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Water
  • 3 tablespoons Olive oil
  • 1 tablespoons Finely chopped flat-leaf parsley
  • 4 teaspoons Lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon Whole grain mustard
  • 2 tablespoons Finely chopped chives
  • 1 tablespoon chopped capers
  • 1 teaspoon Tabasco sauce, to taste
  • Ice cubes

Steps:

  1. 1. In a small bowl, mix shallots, vinegar, and water. Allow the mixture to marinate for 5 minutes. Drain the shallots and discard the liquid, when ready.
  2. 2. Fill a bowl 2/3 of the way with ice cubes. Place a second bowl on top of the first. This will keep the salmon cool during preparation.
  3. 3. Dice the salmon into bite-sized pieces. Place the diced salmon into the chilled bowl.
  4. 4. Add the shallot mix to the bowl containing the salmon. Add salt and pepper for seasoning, and mix contents in the bowl, thoroughly.
  5. 5. Serve immediately with bread, crackers, or salad. The salmon tartare can also be refrigerated, but it is recommended not to keep refrigerated for more than one day.
