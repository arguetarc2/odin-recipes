Lasagna Recipe

Description

This delicious lasagna recipe consists of layers of goodness, alternating between different types of meat and cheeses.

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 lb Ground meat
  • 1 lb Sweet Italian sausage
  • 1 1/2 jars of meat sauce
  • 1/2 cup Minced onion
  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of Salt
  • 16 oz Ricotta cheese
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 lb Mozarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Aluminum foil

Steps:

  1. 1. Cook the ground beef, sweet Italian sausage, and onions together in a pan over medium heat until browned. Add in 1/2 jar of meat sauce to meat mixture and stir on low heat until boiling. Remove pan from heat
  2. 2. Set a large pot of water to boil and add 1 teaspoon of salt. Once the water starts boiling, place the lasagna noodles in the water to cook for 8-10 minutes. Drain the noodles and rinse them with cold water to prevent sticking.
  3. 3. In a mixing bowl, combine the Ricotta cheese with the egg, and ad 1/2 teaspoons of salt.
  4. 4. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  5. 5. In a 9x13 in rectangular baking dish, place enough meat sauce to cover the bottom. Place 6 lasagna noodles over the bottom of the dish, covering the sauce. Spread the Ricotta cheese mixture over the placed noodles until noodles are covered. Add 1/4 cup parmesan cheese.
  6. 6. Repeat Step 5 until baking dish is filled and ingredients have been used.
  7. 7. Bake in the oven, covered with aluminum foil, at 375 degrees F for 45 minutes.
  8. 8. Remove aluminum foil and continue to bake lasagna for 15 additional minutes.
  9. 9. Remove lasagna from oven and allow to sit for 10 minutes for cooling.
